Chihuahuas

I have a dog

His name is Pepe. He is approximately 11 years old. I adopted him in 2015, shortly after moving to the Quad Cities. He has lived in three different homes with me!

He is my best friend that has supported me so much in the last 6 years.

His Looks

He has a deer head and short, tan hair. He has big brown eyes. He has a long body, almost like a weiner dog. He is taller than most chihuahuas I have seen. He is around 11 pounds (He probably needs to lose a pound or two)

His Attitude

He loves to cuddle with me. I think he'd honestly cuddle with anyone as long as there is a blanket involved. He likes to crawl under blankets and lie against people so he is extra warm.

He likes walks. he gets overly exhausted from being very excited in the beginning, but once he calms down we usually get in a 2 mile walk! He is scared of children and some men scare him, but that unfortunately is a common thing with shelter dogs.
